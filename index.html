welcome to my first p5.js program 😝🤭🫡

keys to press:

c - randomly changes the color of sphere

w - changes the color of sphere to white

backspace - exit the loop; pauses the sphere

enter - enter the loop; generates random spheres

press any other keys and the color will stay the same for all the spheres generated until you press 'c' again

on a good day, it runs well. on other days...it's slow & laggy. i ask for you patience!
